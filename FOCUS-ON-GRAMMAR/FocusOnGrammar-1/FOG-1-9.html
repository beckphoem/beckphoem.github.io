STEP 2: GRAMMAR PRESENTATION

IMPERATIVES

Affirmative Negative
Turn left. Don't turn right.
Park here. Don't park there.

GRAMMAR NOTES

1. Imperative

Use the imperative for directions, instructions, requests, and suggestions.

  • Turn left. (direction)
  • Answer the questions. (instruction)
  • Please hand me my jacket. (request)
  • Try this cake. (suggestion)

Use the base form of the verb for the imperative.

  • Open the door.
USAGE NOTE: Please makes a request more polite. Please comes at the beginning or the end of the sentence.
Please help me.
Help me, please.

2. Negative Form of the Imperative

Use do not + the base form for the negative.

  • Do not park here.

Don't is the short form (contraction) of do not.

  • Don't park here.

BRIAN SCUDAMORE: The Business of Junk

BRIAN SCUDAMORE was born in San Francisco in 1970. In 1978, his family moved to Vancouver. Brian wasn't a good student. He didn't finish high school, so colleges didn't want him. But Brian wanted to go to college. He talked to professors at a business college. They liked him, and they offered him a place. Brian enrolled at the college.

College was expensive, and Brian needed money. One day he was at a restaurant. An old truck was in the street outside. Brian looked at all the junk in the truck. He realized something important. People didn't want to move their own junk. They wanted someone else to take it away. So, Brian started his own junk removal business. He moved junk from people's homes. He worked hard, and his business expanded.

Brian wanted an unusual name for his business. He liked the name 1-800-GOT-JUNK?, but the Department of Transportation of Idaho owned that name. That didn't stop Brian. He talked to the Department, and they agreed to give him the name.

Brian was busy with his business, so he never graduated from college. But that wasn't a problem for him. Today he is a very successful businessman.

What is Brian like? Does he love junk and keep it in his house? He says, "No. I'm very neat, and I don't keep many things."

STEP 2: GRAMMAR PRESENTATION

FUTURE WITH BE GOING TO: QUESTIONS

Yes/No Questions

Be Subject Going to + Base Form Short Answers
Am I going to get the job? Yes, you are. / No, you're not. or No, you aren't.
Is the program going to start soon? Yes, it is. / No, it's not. or No, it isn't.
Are we going to move? Yes, we are. / No, we're not. or No, we aren't.

Wh- Questions

Wh- Question Word Be Subject Going to + Base Form Short Answers
When are you going to start? Next Monday.
How are they going to get there? By bus.

Wh- Questions About the Subject

Wh- Question Word Be Going to + Base Form Short Answers
Who is going to help us? I am.

GRAMMAR NOTES

1 Be going to: Yes/No Questions

To make yes/no questions with be going to, put am, is, or are before the subject.

  • Am I going to have a big part?
  • Is he going to change jobs?
  • Are they going to buy a house?

We usually use contractions in negative short answers.

A: Is he going to change jobs?
B: No, he's not. or No, he isn't.

BE CAREFUL! Do not use contractions with affirmative short answers.

A: Are they going to play soccer this weekend?
B: Yes, they are.
NOT Yes, they're.

2 Be going to: Wh- Questions

To form most wh- questions with be going to, use a wh- question word + be + subject + going to + the base form of the verb.

A: When is it going to begin?
B: Next year.

To form a wh- question about the subject, use who or what + is + going to + the base form of the verb.

A: Who is going to be the producer?
B: Dan Evans is.

We usually use short answers for wh- questions with be going to. We can also use long answers.

A: What are you going to do this summer?
B: Study Chinese.
or
B: I'm going to study Chinese.

UNIT 30 REVIEW

C. Correct the conversation. There are five mistakes.

A: I got a new job. I'm going to be a producer.

B: Is you going to work full-time? -> Are you going to work

A: Yes, I'm. -> I am

B: When you are going to start? -> When are you going to

A: Next month.

B: Are you going take a vacation? -> to take

A: Yes, I'm going to go to Toronto next week.

B: Who you going to stay with? -> Who are you going to stay with?

A: My aunt.

Corrected Mistakes:

  1. Is you going to work should be Are you going to work.
  2. Yes, I'm should be Yes, I am (No contractions in affirmative short answers).
  3. When you are going to start? should be When are you going to start? (Word order for Wh- questions).
  4. Are you going take should be Are you going to take.
  5. Who you going to stay with? should be Who are you going to stay with?

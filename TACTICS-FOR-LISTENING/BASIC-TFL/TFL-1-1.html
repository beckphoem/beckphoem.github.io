4. Let's Listen

We usually use a title (Ms., Mr., Mrs., Miss, Dr., Professor) with a last name, but not with a first name, in formal greetings.

Correct

Formal: Good morning, Ms. Smith.

Informal: Good morning, Mary.

Incorrect

❌ Good morning, Ms. Mary.

❌ Good morning, Smith.
